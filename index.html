Our Services

How The Mickies Work

We believe in providing exceptional cleaning services that meet the diverse needs of both residential and industrial clients. Our approach is thorough, reliable, and tailored to every space.

Office Cleaning

From small offices to large corporate spaces, we ensure a clean, safe, and productive environment for your team and clients. Our services include deep cleaning, window washing, and more.

Pool Cleaning

Keep your pool sparkling clean and safe with The Mickies' comprehensive pool cleaning services. We handle everything from debris removal to filter maintenance and water balancing.

Carpet Cleaning

Our professional carpet cleaning service removes dirt, stains, and allergens, leaving your carpets looking fresh and clean. Perfect for both homes and offices.

Kitchen Cleaning

Our deep kitchen cleaning service ensures that your kitchen is spotless and hygienic, from counters to appliances, leaving it ready for cooking and food preparation.

Tile and Grout Cleaning

Our tile and grout cleaning services restore the shine and cleanliness to your floors and walls, removing built-up dirt, grime, and stains. Ideal for both residential and commercial spaces.

Post-Construction Cleaning

After construction or renovation, we provide a comprehensive cleaning service that removes dust, debris, and leftover materials, making the space ready for occupancy or use.
